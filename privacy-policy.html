1Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, as well as information generated by your use of the Platform.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Registration: Full name, email address, phone number, password, and selected role (Buyer/Renter or Seller/Lister)
  • Seller Verification: Government-issued ID, proof of ownership documents (e.g., Deed of Sale), and any supporting documents required for identity verification
  • Property Listings: Property photos, descriptions, pricing, location, and listing details submitted by sellers
  • Messages: Content of messages sent between buyers and sellers through the Platform
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, bio, and any optional details you add to your account

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, search queries, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • IP Address: Logged for security and audit trail purposes
  • Session Data: Login timestamps, session duration, and logout events

2How We Use Your Information

HomeSure uses the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account, authenticate your identity, and provide role-appropriate access
  • Platform Operations: To display property listings, facilitate messaging between users, and process seller verifications
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent listings, unauthorized access, and abusive behavior
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the functionality, design, and performance of HomeSure
  • Communications: To send account-related notifications, verification emails, and important platform updates
  • Compliance: To comply with applicable Philippine laws, respond to legal requests, and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Audit Trail: To maintain records of administrative actions for accountability and security purposes

3Sharing of Information

HomeSure does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Between Users: Seller contact details and listing information are visible to registered buyers. Buyer identities are shared with sellers when a message is initiated.
  • Platform Administrators: Admins and Super Admins may access user information for moderation, verification, and support purposes. Access is logged and audited.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government authority in the Philippines.
  • Safety: We may share information to protect the rights, property, or safety of HomeSure, our users, or the public.
We never sell your personal data. Any data sharing is strictly limited to what is necessary to operate the Platform or comply with legal requirements.

4Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the services you requested.

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained for the duration of your account
  • Deleted Accounts: A 30-day grace period applies after deletion request. After that period, personal data is permanently removed from our systems.
  • Audit Logs: System audit trail entries may be retained for up to 12 months for security and compliance purposes
  • Listing Data: Closed or removed listings may be retained in anonymized form for analytics purposes

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through your Account Settings.

5Data Security

HomeSure implements reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure session management with authenticated role-based access control
  • IP address logging and audit trails for all administrative actions
  • Seller identity verification before allowing property listings to be published
  • Periodic re-verification requirements (every 6 months) for active sellers

While we take data security seriously, no system is completely immune to security risks. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to log out after each session, especially on shared devices.

6Cookies and Local Storage

HomeSure uses browser-based storage mechanisms to enhance your experience:

  • sessionStorage: Used to maintain your login session while you are actively using the Platform. This data is cleared when you close your browser tab.
  • localStorage: Used to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode) across sessions.

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies. You can clear browser storage at any time through your browser settings, which will log you out of the Platform.

7Your Rights

Under the Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012) of the Philippines, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you

Right to Correction

Update or correct inaccurate personal information in your profile

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your account and associated personal data

Right to Object

Object to the processing of your personal data for certain purposes

Right to Portability

Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format

Right to Complain

Lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) of the Philippines

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@homesure.com. We will respond within 15 business days.

8Children's Privacy

HomeSure is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a user under 18 has registered, we will promptly delete their account and associated data.

If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support@homesure.com.

9Third-Party Links

HomeSure may contain links to external websites or services not operated by us. This Privacy Policy applies only to the HomeSure platform. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify registered users via email or in-platform notification

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of HomeSure after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
